Research group

ASTRA Lab

Advanced Systems for Temporal, Multimodal, and Space Research.

ASTRA Lab develops artificial intelligence systems for temporal data, multimodal learning, scientific data generation, and space-weather prediction.

Research

Research Areas

Generative Models

Diffusion models, generative adversarial networks, adversarial autoencoders, and scientific data generation.

Multimodal Learning

Generation and translation across images, text, vector data, and time-series data.

Space Weather Prediction

Machine-learning systems for early prediction of solar flares, solar energetic particle events, and coronal mass ejections.

Time Series Analysis

Temporal modeling, preprocessing, representation learning, and forecasting for scientific time-series data.
